Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Rentify?

To create an account, click on the Sign Up button on the homepage, enter your email and password, and verify your email. Once verified, you can set up your profile and start using Rentify.

How can I list my property for rent?

You can list your property by going to the "Listings" section in your dashboard, clicking "Add New Listing", and filling in the property details, images, and rental price. Once submitted, your property will be live after admin approval.

What payment methods are supported for paying rent?

Rentify supports multiple payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can choose your preferred method at checkout under the Billing & Payments section.

What should I do if I face issues with my account?

If you experience login issues or technical problems, try resetting your password first. If the issue persists, contact our Technical Support Team via the Support section.

How can tenants and landlords communicate on Rentify?

Tenants and landlords can communicate directly using the Rentify Chat System, where they can ask questions, negotiate terms, and schedule property visits securely within the platform.

How does Rentify ensure security and privacy?

Rentify follows strict security measures, including data encryption, two-factor authentication (2FA), and verified user profiles to ensure a safe experience for both landlords and tenants.
